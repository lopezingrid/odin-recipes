Potato Gnocchi

Description

Homemade gnocchi sounds tricky, but is pretty easy. A classic dish of the kitchen in many countries of the world, the irresistible potato gnocchi.

Ingredients (for 4 servings)

  • 1kg of potatoes
  • 300gr of flour 0000
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. The first thing we are going to do wash the potatoes well since we are going to boil them with the skin. This is very important so that later the potato gnocchi do not fall apart, since by cooking them with the peel we prevent them from absorbing excess water. We make a horizontal cut around the contour of the potato, in the video it looks good, and we take them to a pot with water. Cook until they fall off when punctured.
  2. We take them out and we let them cool so as not to burn ourselves. With the cut we made, the shell has to come out very easy in two parts.
  3. We press the potatoes still hot, add salt, pepper and mix super well. We add the beaten egg and the flour. We are not going to add all the flour in one, we are going to save a little to see how it comes. The amounts are rather proportional, it may be that the potato has absorbed more water or that the egg is smaller, so we are going to add the flour little by little.
  4. Join by hand until you can move to the counter and finish integrating. If in this step they see that more flour is needed, we have the rest to add. It is not necessary to love a lot, the idea is to reach a united and uniform mass without touching it a lot.
  5. Cut strips of dough and make rolls, and cut pieces about 2 or 3 cm wide, you can flour the knife to make it easier.
  6. Shape the gnocchi with the gnocchi or fork.
  7. For cooking, bring batches to a pot of boiling salted water. They will see that they fall to the bottom. When they go up, they leave them for 1 minute and they are ready. you can serve it with sauce or cheese!
