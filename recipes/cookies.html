Oatmeal, chocolate and banana cookies

Ingredients (for 4 servings):

  • 3 bananas
  • 120 gr oatmeal
  • Cocoa powder

Description

Today I propose you this wonderful and simple recipe for some delicious oatmeal, chocolate and banana cookies that are really ideal for all of us. Its preparation is fast and proof of any person, even if you are not very skilled or skilled in baking.

Elaboration 2''

Servings 12

Difficulty Medium

Steps

  1. We peel the bananas and mash them in a bowl.
  2. We add the cocoa powder and mix well, and add the oats. We stir until obtaining the consistency of a paste.
  3. We create some balls the size of a coffee spoon. We place them on a baking sheet previously greased with butter or covered with non-stick paper. With our fingers we lightly squash the balls of dough to shape the cookies.
  4. Bake at 180ÂºC for about 20 minutes. Remove from the oven and let them warm before removing the cookies from the tray. Ready!
